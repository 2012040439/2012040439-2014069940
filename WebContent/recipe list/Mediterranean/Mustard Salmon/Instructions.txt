Method:
1) Heat oven to 450ºF. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil. In a small bowl, whisk together the mustard and maple syrup.
2) Place the salmon on the prepared baking sheet and top with the mustard mixture. Roast until the salmon is opaque throughout, 
10 to 12 minutes.

Ingredients
¼ c. whole-grain mustard
2 tbsp. pure maple syrup
1¼ lb. skinless salmon fillet
